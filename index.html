A Bit About Me

Dear Parent,

Dear Parent, I know it sounds a bit strange, a man as a child-minder. To be honest, it was a bit strange for me too, at first. After 20 years as an IT specialist, I found myself more and more in demand to look after the children of my two sisters, so they could go out to work.

And I found I loved it! And I became their Super Uncle.

Soon, their friends were asking if I could mind their children as well, and before I knew it, I was doing less and less IT and more and more child minding. Turns out, it's a very rewarding thing to do!

Since 2017 I've looked after more than 500 children for over 100 families. I've looked after 7 sets of twins ranging in age from 15 months old. I've worked with children of all abilities - autism, ADHD, cerebral palsy, separation anxiety and much more.
